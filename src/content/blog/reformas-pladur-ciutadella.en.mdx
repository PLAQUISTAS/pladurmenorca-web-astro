## How Are Townhouses Renovated in Ciutadella Old Town?

The historic core of Ciutadella preserves traditional Menorcan townhouses with **60 cm marés walls**, timber-beam slabs and layouts from another era. Rehabilitation by new owners (locals and foreigners) seeks to keep the character while adapting comfort.

### Wall linings over marés walls

40-60 cm marés walls offer excellent thermal mass but uneven surfaces without insulation. A **self-supporting drywall wall lining** (Placo, Knauf or Lafarge) is the standard solution: an independent frame separated from the wall, insulation in the cavity and board as the finish.

This regularises the wall, adds insulation (CTE DB-HE compliance) and routes services without chasing. Price: **€35-50/m² installed**.

### Interior space rework

Townhouses have small rooms and long corridors. Rework with drywall partitions creates open living-kitchen-dining spaces without affecting the perimeter marés walls.

Drywall advantages: no added weight (25-35 kg/m² vs 100-150 kg/m² for brick) on compromised timber slabs, dry execution with no moisture affecting beams and total timeline cut by **40-60%** vs traditional construction.

### False ceilings on upper floors

Upper floors with sloped ceilings are levelled with a drywall false ceiling that hides the structure and houses thermal insulation, critical for summer comfort.

## Cala en Bosc and Son Xoriguer: Apartments and Golf

Cala en Bosc and Son Xoriguer are the main coastal cores south of Ciutadella, with a natural harbour, urban beaches and proximity to the golf course. Housing stock of 1980s-90s apartment buildings and villas.

### Holiday-apartment renovations

Apartments 30-50 years old needing comprehensive update. Drywall allows the intervention to be completed in low season and the apartment ready for the next.

Most common interventions:

- Rework (turning 3 small bedrooms into 2 spacious with en-suite): drywall partitions (€45/m²) in 3-5 days.
- False ceilings with integrated LED (€35-47/m²).
- Acoustic insulation between units (DB-HR compliance).

More in [drywall for holiday apartments in Menorca](/en/pladur-apartamentos-turisticos-menorca).

### Acoustic insulation in apartment buildings

1970s-90s tourism-boom buildings with single hollow-brick walls without insulation. Drywall acoustic wall linings with 60 kg/m³ mineral wool + anti-vibration band + double board reach 45-50 dB reduction.

Cala Santandria, Son Oleo and Sa Caleta form the south-coast tourist backbone close to Ciutadella town. Typology: apartment buildings and small beach hotels.

### Hotels and tourist establishments

These hotels are in continuous renovation. Drywall renovations cover compartmentation with acoustic partitions, LED false ceilings, new common areas (spas, meeting rooms, restaurants) and fire protection with fire-rated drywall (EI30/60/120, CTE DB-SI compliance).

## Factors That Affect Cost in the West Coast

### Travel from Maó

Maó-Ciutadella distance: **44 km** by motorway Me-1 (40-50 min). The corresponding logistics cost is reflected transparently within the fixed quote — diluted to a minor share on large renovations (>50 m²).

### Old-town accessibility

Narrow streets make cargo-vehicle access difficult. Drywall boards at 1.20 × 2.60 m require minimum access; in some cases, manual lifting up narrow staircases.

### Seasonal availability

Demand concentrated between October and March. Plan in advance.

## Real Case in Ciutadella: 110 m² Single-Family Home

A family living in Ciutadella centre bought a 110 m² 1970s home with original layout: 4 small bedrooms, long corridor, closed kitchen and deteriorated plaster ceilings. Interior walls of hollow brick without insulation and block façades without lining, with **E energy certificate rating**.

**Goal:** rework (3 generous bedrooms + kitchen open to living room), improve insulation and renew ceilings with LED.

**Work carried out:**

- **Rework**: demolition of 3 old partitions and building of 4 new drywall partitions.
- **Thermal insulation** on the 2 main façades (48 m²): self-supporting wall lining with 50 mm rockwool (40 kg/m³) and 15 mm Placo board.
- **LED false ceilings** across the whole home (110 m²): flat with 48 recessed downlights and perimeter LED strip in the living room.
- **Acoustic insulation** on master-bedroom party wall (14 m²): double board and high-density mineral wool.

**Results:**

- Drywall-line duration: **10 working days** (2 installers)
- Cost: **€9,800** (material and labour)
- Energy certificate: from **E to C rating**
- Estimated cooling/heating savings: **35-40%**
- Outdated, dark layout turned into a modern, efficient space
