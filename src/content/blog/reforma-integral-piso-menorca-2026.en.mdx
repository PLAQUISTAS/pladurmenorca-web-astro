## What a Full Flat Renovation Includes

A full renovation addresses every item in the home: layout, services (electrical, plumbing, HVAC), finishes (floors, walls, ceilings), joinery and finishes. Dry build with drywall (Placo, Knauf or Lafarge boards) articulates the rework, false ceilings and insulated wall linings.

### Main items

The ten items of a full renovation are: demolition and rubble removal, partitioning and layout (drywall partitions from €45/m²), false ceilings (from €29/m² with integrated lighting and HVAC), acoustic and thermal insulation (wall linings with mineral wool, from €7/m² thermal material), electrical (REBT), plumbing, HVAC (ducted or splits), floor finishes, paint and finishes, and joinery.

## Full Renovation by Maó District

The area shapes price, logistics and intervention type.

- **Sant Climent**: 1950s-70s buildings with limited slabs. Drywall (25-35 kg/m²) cuts structural load 75% vs brick partitions (100-150 kg/m²).
- **El Terreno**: old flats with tall ceilings (3.20-3.50 m). Drywall false ceilings with LED restore proportion and improve climate control.
- **Portixol**: beachfront homes exposed to marine humidity. H-type moisture-resistant boards (Placo, Knauf) in bathrooms and kitchens.
- **Son Vida**: luxury villas with premium finish demand and home automation integrated in drywall cavities.
- **Génova**: hillside plots, complex logistics; the boards' light weight eases manual handling through narrow access.

## Factors That Push Up Renovation Costs in Menorca

### Island logistics

Materials carry a **12-18% premium** due to sea freight. Placo, Knauf or Lafarge boards, cement, tiles, joinery and HVAC arrive by ship with longer lead times.

### Labour market

Demand exceeds supply between March and October. Wages for installers, electricians and plumbers are 15-25% above the mainland average.

### Accessibility

Flats in the historic centre with narrow stairs and no lift push up material delivery and rubble removal costs.

### Finish level

The gap between mid-range and high-end can be 60-80%: large-format porcelain, designer tapware, lacquered joinery, architectural lighting.

## Drywall vs Traditional Construction: 40-60% Less Time

In a modern full renovation, drywall articulates the entire internal layout, replacing masonry in partitions, ceilings and wall linings.

Dry build completes the layout-and-ceiling line in **1-2 weeks (7-12 days)**, against 3-4 weeks for traditional construction: a **40-60% timeline saving**. It also generates **80% less waste** (RD 105/2008 compliance), releases the space sooner for other trades and allows parallel work thanks to the absence of water, cement and heavy rubble.

Drywall systems meet CTE DB-HE (energy efficiency), DB-HR (noise protection) and DB-SI (fire safety) requirements.

## How to Save on Your Renovation Without Sacrificing Quality

Define scope well before requesting a quote: changes during the works are the main cause of cost overruns. Group every drywall item into a single intervention (partitions + ceiling + insulation) and avoid demand peaks. Scheduling for November-January can save 5-10% versus the February-May peak. Request at least two detailed, comparable quotes.

## Real Case: 95 m² Flat in Alaior — Full Renovation

A young couple bought a 95 m² flat in [Alaior](/en/pladur-alaior) with an original 3-bedroom layout. Built in the 1980s, it had deteriorated plaster ceilings, uninsulated party walls and a layout unsuited to a family with two children.

**Goal:** convert from 3 to 4 bedrooms, LED false ceilings and acoustic insulation on the party wall.

**Work carried out (drywall line):**

- Rework: demolition of 2 partitions and building of 5 new drywall partitions
- LED false ceilings (95 m²): 42 dimmable recessed downlights, 2 circuits
- Master-bedroom party-wall acoustic insulation (12 m²): double board + 50 mm high-density mineral wool
- North-façade thermal insulation (18 m²): 40 mm rockwool

**Results:**

- Drywall line duration: **12 working days** (2 installers)
- Drywall line cost: **€14,800**
- Property valuation: **+18%** after renovation
- Modern space with 4 bedrooms, integrated lighting and acoustic-thermal comfort

The key was planning: defining the layout upfront let drywall coordinate with electrical works and plumbing with no mid-project changes.
