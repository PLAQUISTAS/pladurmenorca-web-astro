The energy efficiency certificate (CEE) is not just paperwork: it affects sale price, letting, mandatory ETV compliance and access to PIREP (80%) and PREE 5000 grants. This guide explains HULC/CE3X calculation, interventions ranked by impact/cost and real cases in Sant Climent.

## What is the Energy Efficiency Certificate

The CEE has been mandatory since 2013 (RD 235/2013, now RD 390/2021) for sale, letting and ETV (vacation rentals). It is issued by a qualified technician (architect, technical architect or engineer) using HULC (Unified LIDER-CALENER Tool) or CE3X.

The rating runs from A (highest) to G (lowest), based on two indicators:
- **CO2 emissions** (kg CO2/m²·year).
- **Non-renewable primary energy consumption** (kWh/m²·year).

Menorca is in **climate zone B3** (CTE-HE): mild winter, warm summer.

### CEE scale zone B3 (CO2 emissions kg/m²·year, dwellings)

| Letter | Emissions |
|---|---|
| A | <6.5 |
| B | 6.5-10.5 |
| C | 10.5-17.0 |
| D | 17.0-27.5 |
| E | 27.5-43.0 |
| F | 43.0-62.5 |
| G | >62.5 |

### Why it matters

- **Sale/letting:** Idealista and Fotocasa display CEE prominently. G vs C price gap: 8-15%.
- **ETV in Menorca:** mandatory CEE disclosure to Consell Insular de Menorca. ETV with CEE F/G receive lower scoring.
- **Grants:** PIREP (80% grant), PREE 5000, 40% income-tax deduction all require pre- and post-works CEE with improvement over 30%.
- **Bills:** a CEE C consumes 40-60% less than a CEE F.

### Maó housing stock

Over 70% of Maó flats have CEE E, F or G (IBAVI data 2024). Concentration in 1950-1980 buildings: Old Town, Sant Climent, Eixample, Malbúger, Trepucó, Camp Redó, Port de Maó surrounds.

## How the Rating is Calculated

### Official software

- **HULC** (Unified LIDER-CALENER Tool): full zone-by-zone calculation, used for new builds.
- **CE3X**: simplified method for existing buildings. The most widely used in certifying Maó flats.

Inputs: year of construction, envelope U-value, wall/roof composition, frames, glazing U, heating/AC/DHW system, orientation, zone B3, usable area.

### Factors by weight in the rating (zone B3)

1. **Thermal envelope (façades + roof):** 45-55% of the weight. A Placo Therm lining with Isover Arena 60-80 mm cuts U from 1.6 to 0.35 W/m²K.
2. **Frames and glazing:** 15-25%. Aluminium without RPT U = 5.7 W/m²K → RPT + low-e 4+16+4 glass U = 1.4-1.8 W/m²K.
3. **AC/heating/DHW systems:** 15-20%. Air-source heat pump (COP 3.5-4.5) vs electric radiators (COP 1).
4. **Roof on top floor:** 20-30%. A Rockwool Alpharock 80 mm false ceiling cuts U from 2.2 to 0.33 W/m²K.

Conclusion: the Placo Therm lining with insulation is the intervention with the best cost-benefit ratio on the heaviest factor.

## Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Baseline CEE

A qualified technician issues the starting CEE. Mandatory registration with **IBAVI** (Balearic Housing Institute). Cost: €80-150. Time: 2-5 working days. **Essential before works** to access deductions.

### Step 2: Thermal diagnosis

On-site diagnosis by drywall installer: identification of exterior façades, roof, frame U-value, thermal bridges. Proposal with scope, λ and target U.

[Request your visit](/en/presupuesto-pladur).

### Step 3: Quote

Detail: m² of lining, insulation type and thickness (Isover Arena, Rockwool Alpharock, graphite EPS, PUR), Placo/Knauf board, ancillary works (skirting, sockets, PUR at jambs), timescale.

### Step 4: Execution

80 m² flat: **3-6 working days**. Chassis fitting, insulation, boards, joint treatment, paint-ready finish. Critical details: PUR base sealing, insulation returns at window reveals, envelope continuity.

### Step 5: Post-works CEE

New certification and IBAVI registration. Document evidences improvement for deductions and grants.

### Step 6: Deductions and grants

- **Income tax 40%** for CEE improvement over 30% in primary energy consumption (up to €7,500 base, max €3,000 deduction).
- **Income tax 20%** for improvement over 7% in heating+cooling demand.
- **Income tax 60%** in whole buildings with improvement over 30%.
- **PIREP** (Public Buildings Rehabilitation Programme) and **PREE 5000**: up to 80% grant in certain cases.
- **Govern Balear**: Next Generation EU aid 2024-2026.

## Real Case: Flat in Sant Climent, F → C

### Home

75 m², 2nd floor, 1975 building. SW + NE façades. Brick walls with empty cavity (U 1.7 W/m²K). Aluminium without RPT with 4 mm single glazing (U 5.7 W/m²K). Plaster ceiling with no insulation. **Starting CEE: F** (48 kg CO2/m²·year).

### Diagnosis

- Heating demand: 45 kWh/m²·year.
- Cooling demand: 32 kWh/m²·year.
- SW façade solar radiation >5 kWh/m²·day Jun-Aug.

### Intervention

- **SW lining (18 m² living+master bedroom):** 46 mm chassis + Isover Climpor graphite EPS 40 mm (λ 0.031) + 13 mm Placo BA board. Final U: 0.48 W/m²K. **€810**.
- **NE lining (14 m² kitchen+2nd bedroom):** same build-up. **€630**.
- **Window thermal bridges:** 20 mm PUR returns at jambs, lintels and sills on 5 windows. **€350**.

**Drywall total:** €1,790 (material + labour).
**Two CEE (pre + post):** €240.
**Total investment: €2,030.**

### Result

- **CEE: F → C** (3 letters, 62% emissions reduction).
- Climate-energy consumption: 2,100 kWh/year → 1,250 kWh/year (**-850 kWh/year**, ≈€280/year saving).
- **Rent:** rose from €1,020 to €1,150/month (CEE C prime in Sant Climent). +€130/month = **€1,560/year**.
- Payback: **<14 months** (saving + rent).
- With 40% income-tax deduction (€812 refund), payback: **<12 months**.

## Common Mistakes

### Skipping the pre-works CEE

Without a pre-works CEE you cannot evidence the improvement: you lose the 40% income-tax deduction and the PIREP/PREE 5000 grants.

### Insulating only one façade

HULC/CE3X calculates the whole envelope. Treating only SW and leaving NE untreated = 1-1.5 letter improvement vs 2-3 letters with full envelope.

### Ignoring window thermal bridges

Jambs, lintels and sills without insulation returns lose 15-20% of the lining's effectiveness. Sprayed PUR 20 mm or 20 mm Isover strip solves the thermal bridge.

### Thinking window replacement is enough

Windows improve 0.5-1 letter for €4,000-8,000. A Placo Therm lining improves 1-2 letters for €900-1,700. Priority with tight budget.

### Not claiming the tax deduction

Keep itemised invoices (materials + labour), pre- and post-works CEE, and apply on Box 1655/1656/1657 of the tax return. Full guide at [insulation grants in the Balearic Islands 2026](/en/blog/subvenciones-aislamiento-baleares-2026).
