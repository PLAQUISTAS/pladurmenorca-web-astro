## Understanding Noise: Airborne vs Impact

### Airborne noise (conversations, music, TV)

Travels through air and sets the partition vibrating. Measured per UNE-EN ISO 16283-1 as Rw (weighted value in dB). CTE-HR requires Rw ≥50 dB between dwellings.

A Placo Silence lining with double 13 + 13 mm board and Rockwool Alpharock 60 kg/m³ wool reaches Rw 52-58 dB, equivalent to a 24 cm solid-brick wall without the 240 kg/m² of weight.

### Impact noise (footsteps, dragged furniture)

Travels through the solid structure. Measured as L'nT,w in dB (lower L'nT,w = better insulation). CTE-HR DB-HR requires L'nT,w ≤65 dB between dwellings.

Optimal solution: Rockwool 20 mm floating floor at source. From the affected flat: false ceiling with Sylomer SR 18 suspension (ΔL'nT,w -15 to -22 dB).

## Most Common Acoustic Issues in Menorca

### 1960-2008 housing stock with inadequate Rw

**Pre-1997 (no NBE-CA or NBE-CA-88):** 4-5 cm hollow-brick party walls, Rw 33-42 dB. Neighbourhoods: Sant Climent, Malbúger, Trepucó in Maó; old towns of Alaior, Ciutadella, Es Mercadal.

**1997-2008 (NBE-CA-88, mandatory Rw ≥45 dB, actual 42-47 dB):** double-leaf party wall, 9 + 9 cm brick. Punta Prima, Son Bou, Son Xoriguer, Cala en Bosc.

**Post-2008 (CTE-HR DB-HR, Rw ≥50 dB):** party wall system with cavity and mineral wool.

### Tourist noise and hospitality

Punta Prima, Son Bou, Son Xoriguer and Cala en Bosc: indoor nocturnal LAeq 45-55 dBA with windows closed (WHO sleep limit: 30 dBA). Sant Climent and Malbúger: bars and terraces generate LAeq 58-68 dBA from 23:00-03:00 at weekends.

### Holiday lets vs residents

ETV (vacation lets) in mixed communities: frequent acoustic conflicts. Councils like Maó enforce by-laws with LAeq 30 dBA bedroom / 35 dBA living room limits at 23:00-07:00.

## CTE-HR DB-HR: What It Requires in Menorca

| Element | CTE-HR value |
|---|---|
| Between dwellings (Rw) | ≥50 dB |
| Dwelling-common area (Rw) | ≥50 dB |
| Dwelling-commercial premises (Rw) | ≥55 dB |
| Slab impact (L'nT,w) | ≤65 dB |
| Façade in noisy area (D2m,nT,Atr) | ≥37-42 dB |

Applies to post-2008 new builds and full refurbishments. Menorca sits in climate zone B3 and standard CTE-HR acoustic range. Some councils (Maó, Sant Lluís, Es Mercadal) enforce stricter by-laws in tourist areas.

On-site reference tests: UNE-EN ISO 16283-1 (airborne) and UNE-EN ISO 16283-2 (impact).

## Key Materials and Brands

### High-density mineral wool

- **Rockwool Alpharock** 60-100 kg/m³, λ 0.035-0.040 W/m·K. Acoustic benchmark.
- **Isover Arena** 40-70 kg/m³, λ 0.034-0.037. Excellent all-round.
- **Knauf Insulation Acoustifit** 50-60 kg/m³.

Do not confuse with thermal wools of 20-30 kg/m³ (acoustic performance -30%).

### Acoustic boards

- **Placo Phonique** 13 mm: high-density standard acoustic board.
- **Knauf Silentboard** 12.5/17.5 mm: special high-density acoustic board.
- **Glasroc H Ocean** (wet areas): hydrophobic acoustic board for bathrooms.

### Anti-vibration bands and dampers

- **5 mm EPDM** (tracks and junctions).
- **Sylomer SR 18/SR 55** (anti-vibration ceiling suspension).
- **Regupol Sonus Core** (floating floor).

### Viscoelastic membranes

**Acoustiblok 3 mm** or **Tecsound FT 55**: between double boards, convert vibration energy into heat. Effective at low frequencies (<250 Hz).

## Common Mistakes

**1. Thermal wool instead of acoustic:** 20-30 kg/m³ vs 60-70 kg/m³. Performance -30 to -40%.

**2. Not sealing leakage points:** 1 cm² unsealed on a 10 m² wall = -10 dB.

**3. Chassis screwed to the wall without an EPDM band:** kills the decoupling, -8 to -12 dB.

**4. Treating only one wall:** noise finds the untreated flank (flanking transmission through slab and perpendicular walls).
